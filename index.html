Music Commands

  • $play - Play a song or add it to queue 🎶
  • $stop - Stop playing and clear the queue ⏹️
  • $skip - Skip the current song ⏭️
  • $queue [page] - Show the current music queue (paginated) 📜
  • $pause - Pause the current song ⏸️
  • $resume - Resume the paused song ▶️
  • $nowplaying - Show the currently playing song 🎵
  • $volume <0-100> - Set the playback volume 🔊
  • $seek - Seek to a specific time in the current song ⏩
  • $loop - Toggle looping the current song 🔁
  • $shuffle - Shuffle the current queue 🔀
  • $remove - Remove a song from the queue by its position ❌
  • $radioplay - Play a radio station by genre 📻
  • $radio - Play a radio station by genre 📻
  • $playlist - Playlist support (create/view/play) 🎶
  • $lyrics - Get lyrics for a song 🎤
  • $sfx - Play a sound effect in voice 🔊
